Help


  1. Click New Game
  2. Choose Komi and Handicap
  3. Go!
  • The Bot — This is KataGo 1.4.2 running the 20-block net g170e-d96843891. As opposed to Leela, KataGo copes well with large handicaps and can play against variable komi. Try a five stone game!
  • Scoring — KataGo has a concept of territory. The score button will give you the current KataGo estimate.
  • What Would KataGo Do — The Best button shows you the best move according to the AI.
  • Review — Load any sgf file, click First, then keep hitting Next. Look at the Emoji.
  • Analysis and Variations — Click on the Back button to navigate to the position you want to analyze. Click Play repeatedly to see what KataGo would do. The app will automatically start a variation.
    Click 'Back to Main Line' to get out of the variation.
  • Opening — You do not have to start with the empty board. Put any opening you like on the board before clicking Play. You can also preload a game with the Load Sgf button and continue from any point.
